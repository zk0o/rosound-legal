Privacy Policy — RoSound II
Overview
This Privacy Policy describes how RoSound II collects, uses, and handles information when you use the Bot. By using the Bot, you consent to the practices described here.
1. Data We Collect
The Bot may collect and store: Discord User IDs and Server IDs, Roblox account cookies provided voluntarily, upload operation IDs and audio metadata, command usage data, and channel IDs for result notifications.
2. Sensitive Credentials
When you register a Roblox cookie, it is stored on the Bot's server solely to perform upload operations on your behalf. You may remove your cookies at any time using /removecookie. We strongly recommend rotating your Roblox credentials periodically.
3. How We Use Data
Data is used exclusively to perform audio uploads, track operation statuses, deliver results, resume pending operations after restarts, and improve Bot stability.
4. Data Sharing
RoSound II does not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. The only external service your data is sent to is the Roblox API, solely for uploads you initiate.
5. Data Retention
Operation data is retained temporarily and removed once results are confirmed. Cookie data persists until you remove it via /removecookie.
6. Data Removal
Use /removecookie to delete your credentials, or contact the developer through the support server to request full data deletion.
7. Security
We take reasonable measures to protect stored data, but no system is completely secure. By using the Bot you accept the inherent risks of data transmission and storage.
8. Policy Updates
This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
9. Contact
Questions about this policy? Contact the developer through the official RoSound II support server.
